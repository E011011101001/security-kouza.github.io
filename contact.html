Notes on Short-term Visitors and Research Students:
Since faculty members are not stationed in Kyoto, we basically do not accept short-term visitors or research students.

关于短期访问学者和研究生(非正式学生)的说明:
由于教员不在京都居住，研究室一般不接受短期访问学者或研究生(非正式学生)。

Special Notes and Advice on Cryptographic Research:
International students are not allowed to conduct research on encryption. However, they can conduct research on any other exciting topics about cryptography such as digital signatures, multiparty computation, zero-knowledge proofs, and their applications to blockchain apps and any kind of information systems. Please keep this in mind when writing your research proposal.

关于密码学研究的特别说明和建议:
外国学生不允许从事加密方面的研究。除此以外，他们可以选择任何其他密码学相关的有趣课题，如数字签名、多方计算、零知识证明，以及它们在区块链和任何类型的信息系统中的应用。请在撰写研究计划时牢记这一点。
